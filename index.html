Submission Guidelines
  1. Your completion video and raw footage must have all clicks fully audible throughout the entire completion.
  2. You must record and upload a video of your completion to YouTube or another video sharing platform.
  3. Completion videos may not be deleted after they are accepted.
  4. Your record must show the stats on the endscreen (attempts, orbs, etc.).
  5. Your record must be done on the rated version of the level.
  6. During your completion, you may not use any skips that make any section of the level significantly easier.
  7. If a record is accepted on the GDDL, AREDL, or Pointercrate, it is accepted.
