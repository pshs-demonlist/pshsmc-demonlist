Submission Guidelines
  1. Your completion video and raw footage must have all clicks fully audible throughout the entire completion.
  2. You must record and upload a video of your completion to YouTube or another platform, usually one that supports embedding.
  3. Completion videos may not be deleted after they are accepted.
  4. Your record must show the stats on the endscreen (attempts, orbs, etc.).
  5. During your completion, you may not use any skips that make any section significantly easier.
  6. If a record is accepted on the GDDL, AREDL, or Pointercrate, it is accepted.
